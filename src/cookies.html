Quick Summary: We use essential cookies to make the site work. We also use optional analytics and marketing cookies — but only with your consent. You can manage your preferences at any time in the preferences section below.

1What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognise your device on return visits and remember information about your session — such as items in a cart, login status, or language preference.

Cookies are not programs and cannot carry viruses or malware. They simply store data about your browsing behaviour that the website can read on subsequent visits.

In addition to cookies, we may use similar technologies such as web beacons (pixel tags), local storage, and session storage, which function in comparable ways. This policy covers all such technologies.

2How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Keep you signed in to your account during a session
  • Remember your preferences (language, region, display settings)
  • Enable core platform functionality such as the shopping cart and booking flow
  • Understand how visitors navigate our site so we can improve it
  • Measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns
  • Protect the platform against fraud and security threats
  • Provide personalised content and service recommendations

3Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Always Active

These cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function. Without them, you cannot log in, place orders, or use secure areas. They do not track browsing activity beyond what is necessary to provide the service.

  • User authentication and session management
  • Security tokens and CSRF protection
  • Load balancing and server routing
  • Cookie consent preference storage

Functional Cookies

Optional

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalisation. Disabling them may affect some features but will not prevent the core service from working.

  • Remembering your account type and display preferences
  • Storing your last-used service category
  • Remembering your region (Nairobi, Mombasa, Kisumu)
  • Pre-filling form fields on return visits

Analytics Cookies

Optional

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our platform. All data is aggregated and anonymised — we cannot identify you individually from analytics data.

  • Pages visited and time spent per page
  • User journeys and navigation paths
  • Errors and performance issues
  • Device and browser usage statistics

Marketing Cookies

Optional

These cookies track your browsing habits to serve you relevant advertisements on our platform and partner websites. They are only active with your explicit consent.

  • Tracking ad clicks and conversions
  • Building interest-based advertising profiles
  • Retargeting visitors who have left without booking
  • Measuring campaign reach and effectiveness

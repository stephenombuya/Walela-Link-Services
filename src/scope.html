Our Story & Scope

Built in East Africa, for East Africa — Walela Link Services exists to make every kind of service accessible, reliable, and seamless.

Our Mission

Connecting People to
Services That Matter

Walela Link Services was founded on a simple but powerful belief: that every person and business in East Africa deserves access to reliable, professional services — without the frustration of dealing with fragmented, unreliable providers.

We built a platform that unifies logistics, lifestyle, travel, technology, and creativity into one trusted brand. When you come to Walela, you don't need to look anywhere else.

From the first-time entrepreneur shipping a container to the busy professional who needs an errand run, from the couple planning a dream safari to the event organiser who needs a full PA setup — Walela Link is the one call you make.

What We Stand For

Our Journey

How We Got Here

2020
Founded in Nairobi

Walela Link Services launched as a small delivery and errand service, serving the CBD and Westlands corridors with a team of four riders.

2021
Cargo & Logistics Division Launched

Growing demand from import/export businesses led us to launch our full cargo container logistics arm, partnering with Mombasa port agents.

2022
Tourism & Events Expansion

We added tourism itinerary planning and entertainment equipment hire, serving clients in Nairobi, Kisumu, and Mombasa.

2023
ICT & Fashion Verticals

Responding to client requests, we launched ICT equipment sales and our fashion design studio — rounding out our seven service categories.

2024
Digital Platform Launch

Walela Link Services launched its unified digital platform, enabling clients to book, track, and manage all services from a single interface.

2025 →
Regional Expansion

Now operating with 500+ satisfied clients, we are actively expanding into Uganda and Tanzania while deepening our presence across Kenya.
